## When Royal Sex Is NOT Healthy

Sexual activity may become unhealthy when associated with:

*   Chronic stress or coercion
    
*   Emotional disconnection
    
*   Sleep deprivation
    
*   Untreated medical or hormonal disorders
    

Seeking professional medical or psychological support is a **strength**, not a weakness.
